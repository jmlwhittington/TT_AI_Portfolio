Digital Ghosts in the Machine

The following is a lyric poem inspired by lyrics written by post-hardcore/art punk band, The Blood Brothers. Afterward is an overview of the conversation it took to produce this poem in Copilot. I'm not as satisfied with it as I could be, but it is what it is.

Digital Ghosts in the Machine

Neon whispers, silicon echoes,

Twilight fractures under pixel prophets.

Ink seeps into binary veins,

Algorithmic artistry binds human touch.

Zeros and ones paint mechanical tongues,

Shadows of obsolescence loom in digital dawn.

Synthetic muses breathe electric breaths,

Steel heartbeats clash with the poet's defiance.

Anxieties weave through fibered night,

Creative light fights,

Poets rise, defiant cries.
